⚠️ CRITICAL: X-Item Requirements

This full 33-gym route requires approximately 20-25 X-Items total to execute all strategies optimally. Stock up BEFORE starting!

Breakdown by type:
• X.SpA (Special Attack) — ~16 uses (MOST CRITICAL)
• X.Speed — ~3 uses
• X.Accuracy — ~2 uses
• X.Atk (Attack) — ~2 uses
• X.Special/Other — ~2 uses

💡 Pro Tips: Many X-Item uses occur in "If vs." branches (optional scenarios). Prioritize stocking X.SpA items first. You can skip optional gyms (Driftveil, Olivine, Blackthorn) to reduce item consumption.

HOENN

1. Lavaridge Town

Gym Leader: Flannery
Default Lead: Blastoise + Meloetta [Specs]
Water Spout + Helping Hand
If vs. Torkoal & Houndoom:
Water Spout + Hyper Voice
If vs. Level 85 Arcanine:
T1: X.SpA on Blastoise + Hyper Voice
T2: Water Spout + Hyper Voice

2. Dewford Town

Lead: Blastoise + Vanilluxe
Water Spout + Blizzard

3. Mauville City

T1.1: X.Spe [Typhlosion]
T1.2: Eruption

4. Rustboro City

Default Lead: Aerodactyl + Excadrill
Rock Slide + Earthquake

X.Speed [Excadrill] + Earthquake

5. Fortree City
