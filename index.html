HOENN

1. Lavaridge Town

Gym Leader: Flannery
Default Lead: Blastoise + Meloetta [Specs]

Water Spout + Helping Hand
If vs. Torkoal & Houndoom:

Water Spout + Hyper Voice
If vs. Level 85 Arcanine:

T1: Use X.SpA on Blastoise + Hyper Voice
T2: Water Spout + Hyper Voice
Note: Low chance of 4 turns. Heal if Blastoise is damaged.

2. Dewford Town

Gym Leader: Brawly
Lead: Blastoise + Vanilluxe

Water Spout + Blizzard
Note: Heal if Vanilluxe is low HP.

3. Mauville City

Gym Leader: Wattson
Lead: Typhlosion [Specs] + Excadrill

Eruption + Earthquake
If Electrode leads:

T1.1: X.Spe [Typhlosion]
T1.2: Eruption

4. Rustboro City

Gym Leader: Roxanne
Default Lead: Aerodactyl + Excadrill

Rock Slide + Earthquake
If Tyranitar + Probopass / Relicanth:

X.Speed [Excadrill] + Earthquake

5. Fortree City

Gym Leader: Winona
Default Lead: Aerodactyl + Vanilluxe

Rock Slide + Blizzard
If Altaria leads:

X.Accuracy [Aerodactyl] + Blizzard
If Pelipper leads:

T1: Rock Slide + Icy Wind
T2: Rock Slide + X.Atk [Aerodactyl]
T3: Rock Slide + X.Accuracy [Aerodactyl]

⬆ HEAL AFTER HOENN

KANTO

8. Vermillion City

Gym Leader: Lt. Surge
Lead: Meloetta [Scarf] + Typhlosion

Hyper Voice + Incinerate
If Rain or Electrode on field:

Hyper Voice + X.SpA [Typhlosion]

9. Cinnabar Island

Gym Leader: Blaine
T1 Action: Swap Aerodactyl → Vanilluxe
Rock Slide + Water Spout
UNLESS Torkoal + Moltres lead:

T1.1: X.SpA [Blastoise]
T1.2: Water Spout
T2.1: Rock Slide
T2.2: X.Atk [Aerodactyl]

10. Pewter City

Gym Leader: Brock
Lead: Aerodactyl + Excadrill

Rock Slide + Earthquake

11. Saffron City

Gym Leader: Sabrina
Requirement: Full HP Aerodactyl
Lead: Aerodactyl + Excadrill

Rock Slide + Earthquake

12. Celadon City

Gym Leader: Erika
Lead: Aerodactyl [X.SpA applied] + Typhlosion

Rock Slide + Eruption
If Dragonite or Whimsicott leads:

Let Aerodactyl die, switch to Vanilluxe

13. Fuchsia City

Gym Leader: Janine
Important: Switch Meloetta to Choice Scarf before this gym
Lead: Aerodactyl + Typhlosion

Rock Slide + Eruption
Note: After Fuchsia, set Meloetta back to Choice Specs for rest of run.

⬆ HEAL AFTER KANTO
