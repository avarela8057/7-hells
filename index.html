HOENN

1. Lavaridge Town

Gym Leader: Flannery
Default Lead: Blastoise + Meloetta [Specs]

Water Spout + Helping Hand
If vs. Torkoal & Houndoom:

Water Spout + Hyper Voice
If vs. Level 85 Arcanine:

T1: Use X.SpA on Blastoise + Hyper Voice
T2: Water Spout + Hyper Voice
Note: Low chance of 4 turns. Heal if Blastoise is damaged.

2. Dewford Town

Gym Leader: Brawly
Lead: Blastoise + Vanilluxe

Water Spout + Blizzard
Note: Heal if Vanilluxe is low HP.

3. Mauville City

Gym Leader: Wattson
Lead: Typhlosion [Specs] + Excadrill

Eruption + Earthquake
If Electrode leads:

T1.1: X.Spe [Typhlosion]
T1.2: Eruption

4. Rustboro City

Gym Leader: Roxanne
Default Lead: Aerodactyl + Excadrill

Rock Slide + Earthquake
If Tyranitar + Probopass / Relicanth:

X.Speed [Excadrill] + Earthquake

5. Fortree City

Gym Leader: Winona
Default Lead: Aerodactyl + Vanilluxe

Rock Slide + Blizzard
If Altaria leads:

X.Accuracy [Aerodactyl] + Blizzard
If Pelipper leads:

T1: Rock Slide + Icy Wind
T2: Rock Slide + X.Atk [Aerodactyl]
T3: Rock Slide + X.Accuracy [Aerodactyl]

⬆ HEAL AFTER HOENN

KANTO

8. Vermillion City

Gym Leader: Lt. Surge
Lead: Meloetta [Scarf] + Typhlosion

Hyper Voice + Incinerate
If Rain or Electrode on field:

Hyper Voice + X.SpA [Typhlosion]

9. Cinnabar Island

Gym Leader: Blaine
T1 Action: Swap Aerodactyl → Vanilluxe
Rock Slide + Water Spout
UNLESS Torkoal + Moltres lead:

T1.1: X.SpA [Blastoise]
T1.2: Water Spout
T2.1: Rock Slide
T2.2: X.Atk [Aerodactyl]

10. Pewter City

Gym Leader: Brock
Lead: Aerodactyl + Excadrill

Rock Slide + Earthquake

11. Saffron City

Gym Leader: Sabrina
Requirement: Full HP Aerodactyl
Lead: Aerodactyl + Excadrill

Rock Slide + Earthquake

12. Celadon City

Gym Leader: Erika
Lead: Aerodactyl [X.SpA applied] + Typhlosion

Rock Slide + Eruption
If Dragonite or Whimsicott leads:

Let Aerodactyl die, switch to Vanilluxe

13. Fuchsia City

Gym Leader: Janine
Important: Switch Meloetta to Choice Scarf before this gym
Lead: Aerodactyl + Typhlosion

Rock Slide + Eruption
Note: After Fuchsia, set Meloetta back to Choice Specs for rest of run.

⬆ HEAL AFTER KANTO

SINNOH

14. Pastoria City

Gym Leader: Crasher Wake
Lead: Aerodactyl + Meloetta

T2: ALWAYS use X.SpA [Meloetta]
If Sharpedo + Any leads:

Rock Slide + Hyper Voice
If anything but Sharpedo leads:
X.SpA [Meloetta] + Hyper Voice
If rain is up (when Aero is KO'd):
Switch in Vanilluxe

15. Veilstone City

Gym Leader: Maylene
Lead: Aerodactyl + Typhlosion

Use Sunny Day with Aerodactyl. After sun is up, use Rock Slide.

16. Eterna City

Gym Leader: Gardenia
Lead: Meloetta + Typhlosion

Hyper Voice + Eruption

17. Oreburgh City

Gym Leader: Roark
Lead: Aerodactyl + Excadrill
If Excadrill + Gigalith lead:

Rock Slide + Earthquake
If Tyranitar + Excadrill lead:

Protect + Earthquake
Note: Excadrill must have high defensive IVs not to bait EQ. Small chance of surviving with Rock Slide + EQ — needs 31 HP / 31 Def.
If Tyranitar + Probopass lead:

Salamence team — always a 4-turn solve.

18. Sunyshore City

Gym Leader: Volkner
Lead: Meloetta + Blastoise

Hyper Voice + Water Spout
If Lanturn:

Hyper Voice + X.SpA [Meloetta]
Note: Small chance of Meloetta being outsped by some Pokémon — irrelevant, ignore it.

⬆ HEAL AFTER SINNOH

UNOVA

19. Castelia City

Gym Leader: Burgh
Lead: Vanilluxe + Blastoise

Blizzard + Water Spout

[Optional] Driftveil City

Gym Leader: Clay
Lead: Vanilluxe + Blastoise
If sand is up:
X.Speed [Blastoise] + Water Spout, then Vanilluxe uses Icy Wind
If Sableye on field:

X.SpA [Vanilluxe] + Blizzard
If Seismitoad appears T2:

X.SpA [Vanilluxe] + Blizzard
Note: Use Leppa Berries on Water Spout and Blizzard after this gym.

20. Striaton City (Fire – Chili)

Gym Leader: Chili
Lead: Meloetta + Blastoise

Helping Hand + Water Spout
If Ninetales or Typhlosion leads:

X.SpA [Blastoise] + Water Spout

21. Striaton City (Grass – Cilan)

Gym Leader: Cilan
Lead: Meloetta + Typhlosion [Incinerate]

Hyper Voice + Incinerate

22. Striaton City (Water – Cress)

Gym Leader: Cress
Lead: Meloetta + Typhlosion

Hyper Voice + Eruption

23. Opelucid City

Gym Leader: Iris / Drayden
Lead: Vanilluxe + Typhlosion

Blizzard + Eruption

24. Icirrus City

Gym Leader: Brycen
Lead: Aerodactyl + Typhlosion

Use Aero Sunny Day, then Rock Slide + Eruption

25. Mistralton City

Gym Leader: Skyla
Lead: Blastoise [Blizzard] + Vanilluxe [Icy Wind]
If no Whimsicott or Skarmory leads:
Blizzard + X.SpA [Blastoise]
For 3-turn solve guarantee:
Use Double Blizzard whenever possible, or reload PP with Leppas.
If you did Driftveil: Just spam Blizzard + Water Spout.

26. Nimbasa City

Gym Leader: Elesa
Lead: Meloetta + Typhlosion

Hyper Voice + Incinerate (use Eruption when PP allows)
If Ampharos or Milotic:

Hyper Voice + X.SpA [Typhlosion]

⬆ HEAL AFTER UNOVA

JOHTO

27. Goldenrod City

Gym Leader: Whitney
Lead: Meloetta + Typhlosion

Hyper Voice + Eruption
If Minccino + Miltank lead:

Swap both to Aerodactyl + Excadrill

28. Azalea Town

Gym Leader: Bugsy
Lead: Aerodactyl + Typhlosion [Incinerate]

Rock Slide + Incinerate
If Yanmega or Butterfree leads (Quagsire team):

Rock Slide + Eruption
If Beedrill or Forretress leads:

Swap both to Aerodactyl + Excadrill

29. Violet City

Gym Leader: Falkner
Lead: Blastoise + Vanilluxe

Water Spout + Blizzard
If Pelipper or Zapdos:

X.SpA [Blastoise] + Water Spout

30. Mahogany Town

Gym Leader: Pryce
Lead: Aerodactyl + Typhlosion

Use Aero Sunny Day, then Rock Slide + Eruption

31. Cianwood City

Gym Leader: Chuck
Lead: Meloetta + Typhlosion

Hyper Voice + Eruption
If Machamp or Hitmontop [+ Donphan L71]:

T1: Hyper Voice + X.SpA [Typhlosion]
T2.1: Hyper Voice   T2.2: Eruption
For 3-turn solve guarantee:
Always use X.Special T1 to guarantee OHKO on all variants.
Then use Helping Hand if Donphan or Rhyperior are NOT on the field.

[Optional] Extra Gyms — Heal before tackling these

32. Olivine City

Gym Leader: Jasmine
Lead: Blastoise + Typhlosion [Eruption when possible]

Water Spout + Eruption
If Rapidash:

X.SpA [Blastoise] + Water Spout

33. Blackthorn City

Gym Leader: Clair
Lead: Aerodactyl + Meloetta

T1: Always X.SpA [Meloetta]
Let Aerodactyl die → replace with Vanilluxe
